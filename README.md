GeiDeLei henceforth known as "the app" is provided as is. The user interface is intended to be self explanatory and very simplistic. 

The app relies on third party services that are freely available. In this sense the app acts as a browser that comfortably lets you use these web services and interpret the results in a practical and handsfree way. It also means that these services may at any point in time be changed, removed or updated as they are not part of the app itself. 

GeiDeLei provides instant handsfree translation of a single word or short sentence. Just tap the microphone button and it will listen continously.

Set it to "Do a flip" and enjoy dialogue with yourself. Say a word in your language, hear the translation and repeat it back. Easy learning of new languages and completely handsfree. Supports many languages.

The app uses 3 different translation services for you to chose from. If one service is unavailable another one can take its place.

You can select the voice for each language through the iPhones own System Settings / Accessibility / Read & Speak / Voices and

You can set the speed of the translated voice in the UI

You can record your dialogue with the app and play it back to perfect your pronounciation and hear your improvements

You can tap the translated result to hear the word or phrase again

You can tap the transliteration to see a popup with the full word or sentence and hear the translation played back so you can imitate the pronounciation

The app caches 65535 most recent words or phrases so you can use it offline and limit the number of API lookups

As app developer the goal is to keep the app connected to the best available free services to provide translation at low cost.
